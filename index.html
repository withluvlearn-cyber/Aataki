Good to Know

Frequently asked questions

What does "24-hour fresh" actually mean?+
We don't grind your atta until you order it. Once you do, we stone-grind it and deliver it to your door within 24 hours of milling — so it always reaches you fresh, never a day older.
Why is freshly ground atta better?+
Flour starts losing nutrition and flavour the moment it's milled. Most store-bought atta is months old. Fresh-ground atta retains more nutrients, natural moisture and aroma — which is exactly why your rotis turn out softer and tastier.
Is Aataki really preservative-free?+
Yes — 100%. Because we grind to order and deliver fresh, we never need preservatives or chemicals. Nothing added, nothing taken away.
Where do you deliver?+
We currently deliver fresh across Bhopal. Order today and receive it within 24 hours.
Which variant should I choose?+
For the softest, sweetest rotis, go Premium or Gold (Sharbati wheat). For dependable everyday rotis, choose Classic. On a budget? Basic gives you fresh-ground quality at the best price.
